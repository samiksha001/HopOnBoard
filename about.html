Our Mission

At HopOnBoard, our mission is to make public transportation more accessible and convenient for everyone. We aim to provide real-time tracking of buses, accurate ETA information, and scenic route options to enhance your travel experience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize the way people use public transportation by integrating technology and data to offer seamless, reliable, and enjoyable bus journeys. We strive to be a trusted partner for commuters, ensuring that their daily travels are smooth and efficient.

Our Team

HopOnBoard was developed by Saakshi Dewangan and Samiksha Samal, two passionate computer science students at university. Both are full-stack developers with a keen interest in solving real-world problems through technology. Saakshi and Samiksha have contributed extensively to both the front-end and back-end development of HopOnBoard.

Saakshi Dewangan

Saakshi is a dedicated full-stack developer with expertise in both front-end design and back-end development. She ensures that the interface of HopOnBoard is intuitive and user-friendly while also contributing to the server-side logic to ensure the app runs smoothly.

Samiksha is also a skilled full-stack developer who specializes in creating seamless user experiences from the front-end to the back-end. She ensures that HopOnBoard provides real-time updates and accurate information, integrating complex algorithms and databases with an engaging user interface.

Together, Saakshi and Samiksha have combined their skills to bring HopOnBoard to life, demonstrating the power of collaboration and innovation in solving everyday challenges.
